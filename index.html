<h1>Forest Ecosystem Management Simulation</h1>

<p>Welcome to the Forest Ecosystem Management Simulation. You are a forest manager responsible for maintaining the health of a forest ecosystem. Your decisions will affect biodiversity, forest health, and the needs of the local community. Make your choices wisely!</p>


<h2>Scenario</h2>

<p>The forest is facing several challenges, including invasive species, forest fires, and the needs of the local community for resources. Your goal is to balance these challenges to maintain a healthy ecosystem.</p>


<div class="decision">

    <h3>Decision 1: Invasive Species</h3>

    <p>An invasive plant species has been spreading rapidly in the forest, outcompeting native plants.</p>

    <div class="tooltip">What are invasive species?

        <span class="tooltiptext">Invasive species are non-native organisms that cause harm to the environment, economy, or human health. They can outcompete native species for resources, leading to a loss of biodiversity.</span>

    </div>

    <p>How will you address this issue?</p>

    <button onclick="handleDecision1('remove')">Remove invasive species manually</button>

    <button onclick="handleDecision1('chemical')">Use chemical herbicides</button>

    <button onclick="handleDecision1('ignore')">Ignore the problem</button>

</div>


<div id="decision2" class="decision" style="display:none;">

    <h3>Decision 2: Forest Fires</h3>

    <p>There is an increased risk of forest fires due to dry conditions and accumulated dead wood.</p>

    <div class="tooltip">Why are forest fires important?

        <span class="tooltiptext">Forest fires are a natural part of many ecosystems and can help clear out dead material, promote new growth, and maintain ecological balance. However, they can also be destructive, especially if they get out of control.</span>

    </div>

    <p>How will you manage the risk of forest fires?</p>

    <button onclick="handleDecision2('controlled')">Conduct controlled burns</button>

    <button onclick="handleDecision2('clear')">Clear dead wood</button>

    <button onclick="handleDecision2('no_action')">Take no action</button>

</div>


<div id="decision3" class="decision" style="display:none;">

    <h3>Decision 3: Community Needs</h3>

    <p>The local community relies on the forest for resources like timber and recreational space.</p>

    <div class="tooltip">Sustainable resource use

        <span class="tooltiptext">Sustainable resource use involves managing resources in a way that meets current needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. This includes practices like selective logging and maintaining recreational areas.</span>

    </div>

    <p>How will you balance the community's needs with forest health?</p>

    <button onclick="handleDecision3('selective')">Implement selective logging</button>

    <button onclick="handleDecision3('recreation')">Develop recreational areas</button>

    <button onclick="handleDecision3('exploit')">Allow unrestricted resource use</button>

</div>

    <h2>Outcome</h2>

    <p id="outcomeText"></p>

<script>

    function handleDecision1(choice) {

        document.getElementById('decision2').style.display = 'block';

        if (choice === 'remove') {

            alert("You chose to remove the invasive species manually. This is labor-intensive but avoids chemical impacts on the ecosystem.");

        } else if (choice === 'chemical') {

            alert("You chose to use chemical herbicides. This is effective but may harm non-target species and pollute water sources.");

        } else {

            alert("You chose to ignore the problem. The invasive species may continue to spread and further harm native biodiversity.");

        }

    }


    function handleDecision2(choice) {

        document.getElementById('decision3').style.display = 'block';

        if (choice === 'controlled') {

            alert("You chose to conduct controlled burns. This reduces fire risk and promotes new growth but requires careful planning.");

        } else if (choice === 'clear') {

            alert("You chose to clear dead wood. This reduces fire risk but removes habitat for some species.");

        } else {

            alert("You chose to take no action. The risk of uncontrolled forest fires remains high.");

        }

    }


    function handleDecision3(choice) {

        document.getElementById('outcome').style.display = 'block';

        let outcomeText = "";

        if (choice === 'selective') {

            outcomeText = "You implemented selective logging. This balances resource use with forest health, maintaining biodiversity and providing community resources.";

        } else if (choice === 'recreation') {

            outcomeText = "You developed recreational areas. This supports community well-being and promotes conservation awareness.";

        } else {

            outcomeText = "You allowed unrestricted resource use. This may lead to overexploitation, harming the forest ecosystem and diminishing resources for future generations.";

        }

        document.getElementById('outcomeText').innerText = outcomeText;

    }

</script>
